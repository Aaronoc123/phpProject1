The gym is a tool used by many people to improve health and fitness.
Physical fitness is generally achieved through exercise. Physical fitness is a state of health and well-being and, more specifically, the ability to perform aspects of sports , occupations and daily activities. Physical fitness is generally achieved through proper nutrition, moderate-vigorous physical exercise, and sufficient rest.

Some people are intimidated by the sound of going to the gym but you need to make a plan to help get on track. The most important element to health and fitness is NUTRTION.People believe that the key to being in great shape is exercise ,however in fact it is on the basis Exercise-20% Nutrition-80% .Once you master the concept of this you are on your way!!!

The gym can be used to help people workout using various machines & Equipment which may not be accessible to them.
