Personal History
  • 2006-Present
  • In 2006 , I joined Glen Rovers GAA club. At the time I had a general interest in sport but it was my parents decision because I was too young to decide. Throughout the years my passion for the club continued to flourish.
  • I have many great memories at the club and expect many more in the future. My fondest memory has to be when we won the Premier 1 Minor County Hurling Championships after beating Midleton , who at the time were deemed unbeatable. We overcame Midleton on a scoreline of Glen Rovers 0-19, Midleton 1-11 to win the Cup.
