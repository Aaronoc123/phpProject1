Summer 2018

I went on holidays with three of my friends in the Summer (David,Shane and Jack).
We needed a well deserved sun holiday after completing our Leaving Certificate.In our view it was a great idea to go in the middle of July.

Day-By-Day

On our first day we arrived very early in the morning.We were fairly tired after being up during the night and partially due to the flight .
We got a bus from the airport to our hotel-Surfing Ona Playa. Our hotel was roughly a 5 minute walk from the beach.
The first day was mainly spent organising our rooms and getting familiar with the location we were in.We got food and had an early night.
The next day we decided to take a trip to the Waterpark (Western Park). It was a really good activity as it passed away the time.
In the remainder of the time we spent on the holiday we visited Magaluf,which was a town bordering Santa Ponsa.

We also went on a boat trip which in my view was one of the most enjoyable activities.
We also played Pitch and Putt , which was really enjoyable.
Our Final days were spent going out to nightclubs and shopping.

We really enjoyed the holiday and plan to return in the future as the weather was outstanding.
