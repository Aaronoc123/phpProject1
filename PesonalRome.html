Transition Year

In Transition Year,I visited Rome with my school (Colaiste an Chroi Naofa). We spent 4 days in the ancient city.We chose as a class group to visit here because of its vast historic attractions. In the end it worked out to be an unforgettable experience.

During our time we managed to visit:

  • Fontana di Trevi, one of the top Rome's attractions that is never to be missed.
  • The Colosseum-Originally known as the Flavian Amphitheatre
  • The Vatican City-Enclosing St Peter's Basilica and the vatican museums.
  • The Pantheon-Originally dedicated as a temple to "all the gods"

It was a remarkable experience and I would highly recommend visiting the city.
I hope to return in the future if the chance arises.
